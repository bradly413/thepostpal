## Longer walkthrough (send if someone gets stuck)

### 1. Accept the Facebook tester invite
- Log into facebook.com with the account that manages your business Page.
- Check notifications for "Brad Nichols invited you to test Posterboy Social," or go to **developers.facebook.com/requests**.
- Accept. Without this step, the connect in step 3 gets blocked by Facebook.

### 2. Create your Posterboy account
- Go to **posterboysocial.com/signup**.
- The setup asks about your business and how you sound. Answer like you'd talk to a customer, not like a marketing form. This is what the captions are built from.

### 3. Connect Facebook and Instagram
- From the dashboard, go to **posterboysocial.com/dashboard/connect/meta**.
- Click Connect, log in with the same Facebook account from step 1.
- **Leave every permission checked.** Unchecking any of them breaks publishing or analytics later.
- Pick the business Page. If your Instagram is linked to that Page, it comes along automatically.
- Instagram not showing? In the Instagram app: Settings → Account type and tools → Switch to professional account, then link it to your Facebook Page (Edit Page → Linked accounts). Free, takes two minutes.

### 4. Publish your first post
- Open the Studio, describe what's going on, and let it generate.
- Review the image and caption. Edit anything. Nothing publishes without your approval.
- Publish now, or pick a time. Scheduled posts go out within about 5 minutes of the time you set.
- Go look at your real Page and confirm it landed.

### 5. What Brad needs from you
- Anything confusing, even small stuff.
- Captions that don't sound like you (paste the caption and say what's off).
- Anything broken, with a screenshot if it's quick.
- Text or email whenever, no formal anything.
