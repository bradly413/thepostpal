## Executive summary

The platform core is in good shape: tests are green (97/97), `tsc` is clean, prod smoke passes 10/10, RLS/tenancy is comprehensive and fail-closed, the warm-light design migration is essentially complete, and every finding from the 2026-06-12 audit held (R1–R8, H1–H8 fixed). **Not ship-ready for trust-sensitive scheduled publishing**: two P0s sit in the publish pipeline — (1) "Schedule for later" from the editor/calendar publishes Instagram posts **immediately** and then shows them as queued forever, and (2) the cron has no claim step, so a slow batch or a failed status-write can **double-publish** to a client's real page. A cluster of P1s compounds the same theme: failed publishes are invisible in the UI (a real failed post exists in prod demo right now), "both"-platform publishes aren't atomic, and superadmin is granted off an unverified signup email. Fixes for the top 4 are all S/M and localized.
